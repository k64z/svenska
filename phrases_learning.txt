Vad säger du? - What do you say? (=What do you think?)
Det är bara det att... - "It's just that..." (common way to introduce explanations)
Så är det bara - "That's just how it is" (acceptance of situation)
Det spelar ingen roll - "It doesn't matter" (lit. "it plays no role")
Vad händer? - "What's happening?" (casual greeting, not literal)

Förresten - "By the way" (very common, changes topic)
I alla fall - "Anyway/at least" (conclusion or minimal positive)
Det kommer sig av att... - "It's because..." (formal explanation)
Med andra ord - "In other words"
Å andra sidan - "On the other hand"

Enligt mig - "According to me" (more formal than "jag tycker")
Jag håller med - "I agree" (lit. "I hold with")
Det stämmer - "That's right/correct"
Så klart - Of course
Absolut inte - Absolutely not

Så småningon - "Eventually/gradually" (indefinite future)
För tillfället - "For the time being"
Lagom - "Just right/appropriate amount"
Hinna med - "Have time for/manage to do"

Jag har tråkigt - "I'm bored"
Det gör ingenting - "It doesn't matter/no problem"
Vad synd - "What a pity/shame"
Tur att... - "Lucky that"

Ont om tid - "Short on time"

Hur mår du? - How are you?
